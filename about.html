About me

I'm a passionate developer (and these days tech lead) who takes a great interest in my field. Originally from Copenhagen, I moved to Berlin in March '15 and am loving it here. I've been programming mainly ruby since 2012, and while I've stuck with mostly ruby (and the occasional javascript and elixir) I've gotten quite a bit around in different types of systems. I engage in whatever product I'm working on, and am motivated by having impact on people through tech that I can be proud of. I'm most at home when I'm learning and evolving.

In my free time I have a broad variety of interests, I play piano, read books, play board- and computergames, and I have a little gerbil, McLovin. I'm a pretty easy guy to strike a conversation with, and even though I still don't know a sea of people in Germany, I find myself trying to keep up with social life too.
